Discover Idukki

Idukki, located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Known for its lush green landscapes, wildlife sanctuaries, and the majestic Idukki Arch Dam, the region offers a perfect blend of serenity and thrill.

Visit the hill stations of Munnar and Thekkady, located in close proximity to Idukki, and indulge in the aroma of tea plantations and spice gardens. The cool climate and picturesque landscapes make it an ideal retreat.
